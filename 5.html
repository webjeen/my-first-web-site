AP Precalculus

Advanced Placement (AP) Precalculus is a future Advanced Placement precalculus course and examination, offered by the College Board, in development since 2021[1] and announced in May 2022.[2] The course is set to debut in the fall of 2023, with the first exam session taking place in May 2024. The course and examination are designed to teach and assess precalculus concepts, as a foundation for a wide variety of STEM fields and careers, and are not solely designed as preparation for future mathematics courses such as AP Calculus AB/BC.[3]
